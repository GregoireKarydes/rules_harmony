Privacy Policy

This app uses your device's microphone. This policy explains why and how we use this permission.

1. General Information

This privacy policy describes how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services.

2. Microphone Usage

2.1 Why We Use the Microphone

Our app uses the android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO permission only for:

  • Real-time voice transcription: Convert your speech into text
  • Voice recognition: Understand and process your spoken commands
Important: No audio recordings are stored. Audio is used solely for real-time transcription and immediately deleted.

2.2 How We Use Audio Data

  • Real-time processing: Audio is analyzed instantly for transcription
  • No storage: Audio data is never saved
  • Immediate deletion: Audio is deleted after transcription
  • User control: You can stop transcription anytime

3. Data Storage and Transmission

3.1 No Audio Storage

  • Audio captured by the microphone is processed in temporary memory only
  • No audio files are created or stored on your device
  • Only transcribed text may be temporarily stored based on your settings
  • Audio is automatically deleted after each transcription session

3.2 Transmission

  • Local processing: Whenever possible, transcription is done on your device
  • If transmission is required: Audio is sent securely and encrypted for transcription
  • Server deletion: Audio sent is deleted immediately after transcription
  • No third-party storage: No external service stores your audio data

4. Your Rights and Controls

  • System permission: You can revoke microphone access in Android settings
  • In-app controls: Enable/disable voice transcription in the app
  • No recordings: Nothing is saved, so nothing to delete
  • Transcribed text: Manage or delete transcriptions as needed
  • Immediate stop: Stop transcription anytime

5. Security

  • Encryption of stored data
  • Limited access to audio data
  • Automatic deletion of temporary data
  • Regular security audits
